The V10

A V10 engine is a ten-cylinder piston engine where two banks of five cylinders are arranged in a V configuration around a common crankshaft. V10 engines are much less common than V8 and V12 engines, but are very common in many sport cars such as an audi R8, a Ferrari, or a Lamborghini.
